SLEEVE
Handcrafted For Wacom Intuos Tablet
It can fit tablet perfectly and prottect it from dust and scratches. With thin padding and strong stitches. Valcro in the opening to protect tablet from slipping out accidentally.
Fabric used- Pure cotton, Liinen and slik.
Natural dyed with An- natto, Onion, Marigold, turmeric, Coffee and Tea. Dimensions- 7.5 x 11 x 0.5 cm Wash - Hand wash only and hang from drying in the shade.
The idea behind the project was to create a product from natural dyed fabric with the knowledge acquired in the subject application of natural dyes. The product created is Sleeve for Graphic tablet. First, the fabrics were dyed using differnt natural dyes and then the design process was followed to reach the desired outcome. After finishing the product it was photographed and firther processed for digital presentation.
